## Recommended Sizes:
- **Main logos**: Height ~200-250px (ZebraGo displayed at 70px, BSCC at 65px)
- **Social icons**: 64px × 64px (displayed at 28px × 28px)
- Use transparent PNG format for best results
- Logos will maintain aspect ratio automatically
- Social icons will be white on green background by default, full color on hover
